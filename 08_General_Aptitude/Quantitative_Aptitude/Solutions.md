### Q1. Percentage Increase (GATE 2024) ‚≠ê

**Question:**  
A number is increased by 20% and then decreased by 20%. The net change is:

(A) No change  
(B) 4% decrease  
(C) 4% increase  
(D) 2% decrease

**Solution:**

**Method 1: Formula approach**

Let original = 100

After 20% increase: 100 + 20 = 120

After 20% decrease on 120: 120 - (20% of 120) = 120 - 24 = 96

Net change: 100 - 96 = **4 decrease** = **4% decrease**

**Method 2: Direct formula** ‚ö°

When x% increase followed by x% decrease:
$$
\text{Net change} = -\frac{x^2}{100}
$$

Net change = -20¬≤/100 = **-4%** (negative = decrease)

**Answer: (B) 4% decrease**

**Memory:** "Increase then decrease = Always net loss = x¬≤/100"

### Q2. Time and Work (GATE 2024) ‚≠ê‚≠ê

**Question:**  
A can complete a work in 12 days and B can complete it in 18 days. They work together for 4 days, then A leaves. How many more days will B take to complete the remaining work?

(A) 6 days  
(B) 7 days  
(C) 8 days  
(D) 9 days

**Solution:**

**Step 1:** Work done per day
- A's 1 day work = 1/12
- B's 1 day work = 1/18

**Step 2:** Combined work per day
$$
\text{Combined} = \frac{1}{12} + \frac{1}{18} = \frac{3+2}{36} = \frac{5}{36}
$$

**Step 3:** Work done in 4 days together
$$
\text{Work done} = 4 \times \frac{5}{36} = \frac{20}{36} = \frac{5}{9}
$$

**Step 4:** Remaining work
$$
\text{Remaining} = 1 - \frac{5}{9} = \frac{4}{9}
$$

**Step 5:** Time for B to complete remaining
$$
\text{Time} = \frac{\text{Remaining work}}{\text{B's rate}} = \frac{4/9}{1/18} = \frac{4}{9} \times 18 = 8 \text{ days}
$$

**Answer: (C) 8 days**

### Q3. Speed-Distance-Time (GATE 2023) ‚≠ê‚≠ê

**Question:**  
A train travels the first 100 km at 50 km/h and the next 100 km at 75 km/h. The average speed for the entire journey is:

(A) 60 km/h  
(B) 62.5 km/h  
(C) 65 km/h  
(D) 70 km/h

**Solution:**

**For equal distances at different speeds, use harmonic mean:**

$$
\text{Average speed} = \frac{2xy}{x+y}
$$

where x = 50 km/h, y = 75 km/h

$$
= \frac{2 \times 50 \times 75}{50 + 75} = \frac{7500}{125} = 60 \text{ km/h}
$$

**Verification:**
- Time‚ÇÅ = 100/50 = 2 hours
- Time‚ÇÇ = 100/75 = 4/3 hours
- Total time = 2 + 4/3 = 10/3 hours
- Average = 200/(10/3) = **60 km/h** ‚úì

**Answer: (A) 60 km/h**

**Memory:** "Equal distances ‚Üí Use 2xy/(x+y)"

### Q4. Profit and Loss (GATE 2023) ‚≠ê

**Question:**  
An article is sold at 25% profit. If both CP and SP are reduced by ‚Çπ100, the profit would be 33.33%. The original CP is:

(A) ‚Çπ300  
(B) ‚Çπ400

**Solution:**

**After reduction:**
- New CP = x - 100
- New SP = 1.25x - 100

**Profit = 33.33% = 1/3:**
$$
\frac{\text{New SP - New CP}}{\text{New CP}} = \frac{1}{3}
$$

$$
\frac{(1.25x - 100) - (x - 100)}{x - 100} = \frac{1}{3}
$$

$$
\frac{0.25x}{x - 100} = \frac{1}{3}
$$

$$
0.75x = x - 100
$$

$$
x = 400
$$

**Answer: (B) ‚Çπ400**

### Q5. Ratio and Proportion (GATE 2022) ‚≠ê

**Question:**  
If x : y = 3 : 4 and y : z = 5 : 6, then x : z is:

(A) 3 : 5  
(B) 5 : 8

**Solution:**

**Method: Make middle term (y) equal**

x : y = 3 : 4

Multiply first ratio by 5: x : y = 15 : 20
Multiply second ratio by 4: y : z = 20 : 24

Now y is same (20), so:
**x : y : z = 15 : 20 : 24**

Therefore, **x : z = 15 : 24 = 5 : 8**

**Answer: (B) 5 : 8**

**Shortcut:** x/z = (x/y) √ó (y/z) = (3/4) √ó (5/6) = 15/24 = 5/8

### Q6. Simple Interest (GATE 2022) ‚≠ê

**Question:**  
A sum of money doubles itself in 10 years at simple interest. The rate of interest is:

(A) 8%  
(B) 10%  
(C) 12%  
(D) 15%

**Solution:**

Let Principal = P

After 10 years, Amount = 2P

So, Simple Interest = 2P - P = P

**SI formula:**
$$
SI = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100}
$$

$$
P = \frac{P \times R \times 10}{100}
$$

$$
1 = \frac{R \times 10}{100}
$$

$$
R = \frac{100}{10} = 10\%
$$

**Answer: (B) 10%**

**Memory:** "Doubles in T years ‚Üí R = 100/T%"

$$

**Answer: (B) 24**
